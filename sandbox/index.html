Welcome to the Sandbox

This is where we share early versions of our tools — not slick products, but transparent, practical helpers built for scientists. They’re grounded in FAIR principles and designed to support human-centric science, not replace it. Try ideas early, see how they behave, and help shape where they go. This staged approach aligns with the EU AI Act’s goals for safe, transparent AI development.
