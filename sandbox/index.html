Sandbox

A sandbox is a space we share early mock-ups and lite MVPs — minimal, conceptual, and sometimes still tuning data integration. These tools are tested enough to explore, but not yet ready for full-scale validation. This staged approach aligns with the EU AI Act’s goals for safe, transparent AI development.
