Let's Play

A small corner where we gamify ideas — light, fun, and inspired by living systems. These interactive projects explore the human side of science: curiosity, intuition, and low-pressure experimentation. They sit beside our scientific tools as creative sparks, because that's what being human is.

Lab Survival

Play Now
A fast, K-pop inspired game that teaches proper cell culture habits. Manage CO₂ levels, avoid contamination, and complete timed lab challenges — a playful take on real wet-lab workflows.

Educational Potential

While these began as creative experiments, each mirrors real laboratory intuition. With refinement, they could evolve into lightweight teaching tools for students, interns, and new lab users:

  • Hepatocyte Cell Avatar introduces basic ideas of cell health, energy balance, and homeostasis.
  • Lab Survival reinforces proper culture technique — timing, contamination awareness, and workflow discipline.
