Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously.

We do not collect personal data unless you choose to provide it.

If you submit an email address through our Google Form, it is stored securely on Google’s infrastructure and used only to provide occasional product updates. We do not share or sell your information. You may contact us at any time to request removal.

Our website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools.

Some of our interactive tools (e.g., Sandbox and Play apps) are hosted on third-party platforms such as Streamlit Cloud or Netlify. These services may collect basic technical information (e.g., IP address or usage logs) as part of their normal operation. AvatarsBio does not access or store this information.

If you use AI features inside our demos, only the text you type is sent securely to the AI service to generate a response. We do not store this data, and it is not used to train future models.
