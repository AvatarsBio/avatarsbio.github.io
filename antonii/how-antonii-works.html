How Antonii Works — 4 Simple Steps

Each step addresses a common experimental challenge — and you can use them independently.

1) Focus your variables

Too many variables? Tight budget? Not sure what matters most?
Antonii helps you narrow scope based on your goal — plan smarter from the start.

2) Check the design

Worried about missing controls or hidden confounding?

Too many combinations? Unsure what design to use?
